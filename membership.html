Friends of KC Dirt Community

We offer an annual membership to KC Dirt Community. Membership dues cover expenses related to insurance and special events, such as our summer cookout, holiday party, and other occasions along the way. Membership funds are used solely for our community.

Membership is $20 per rolling year. If you would you like to contribute, you may use the link below for UltraSignup. Thank you to everyone who continues to make this group so inclusive, supportive, and fun. This is what makes KC Dirt Community so special.
