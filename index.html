Who We Are

On the surface, KC Dirt Community is a female-led trail running group in Kansas City. We assemble every Monday night for a group run... rain or shine, snow or heat. Once you start hanging around with us, you’ll realize how much more there is to it.

We are a community that is deeply in love with the trails and the way running brings people together. Although we are female-led, we are all-inclusive. We are motivated to share experiences and introduce our sport to anyone who is interested.

Whether you’ve been running trails for years, are veering off the pavement to try something new, or are running for the very first time, you’ll find a welcoming group in KC Dirt Community. Come on out, and see where the trail takes you.
