    Trails with Friends

    A female-led, inclusive trail running group

KC Dirt Community is a female-led, inclusive trail running group in Kansas City. We assemble every Monday night for a group run... rain or shine, snow or heat. Once you start hanging around with us, you’ll realize how much more there is to it.

We are a community that enjoys spending time on the trails and the way running brings people together. We are motivated to share experiences and introduce our sport to anyone who is interested.

Whether you’ve been running trails for years, are veering off the pavement for the first time, or are a new runner, you’ll find a welcoming group in KC Dirt Community.
