Group Runs

6:00 P.M. Every Monday Shawnee Mission Park, Marina Lot

Monday Night Run

KC Dirt Community meets each Monday evening at 6:00 P.M. in Shawnee Mission Park. We run a loop on the mountain bike trails with 2.5 and 4.5 mile options. If the dirt trails are closed due to wet conditions, we meet at the same time and place to run paved trails.

Runs are female led, with all people and paces welcome. No one gets left behind! Updates are posted each Monday on our Facebook page.

We currently request that people who join our runs wear masks before and after the run and maintain appropriate distance at all times.
