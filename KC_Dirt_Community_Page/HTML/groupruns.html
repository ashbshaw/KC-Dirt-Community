Group Runs

6:00 P.M. Every Monday Shawnee Mission Park, Marina Lot

Monday Night Run

KC Dirt Community meets each Monday evening at 6:00 P.M. in Shawnee Mission Park. We run a loop on the mountain bike trails with a 2.5 and 4.5 mile option. If the mountain bike trails are closed due to wet conditions, we meet at the same place and time to run paved trails.

Runs are female led, with all people and paces welcome. No one gets left behind! Updates are posted each Monday on our Facebook page.

12:00 P.M. Every Wednesday Second Best Coffee

Wednesday Run

We have a group run every Wednesday at noon, starting at Second Best Coffee in Waldo and running an out and back on the Trolley Trail. All paces welcomed.

Please check our Facebook page on Wednesdays for updates.

Saturday Mornings Various Trails in the Kansas City Area

Saturday Morning Run

KC Dirt Community members and friends often meet on Saturday mornings for long runs. Please check the Facebook page for details.

If you would like company on your Saturday morning run, feel free to post on our page or arrange to meet up with other KC Dirt Community members!
